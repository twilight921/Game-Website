Game Overview

Fracturepoint is a MOBA-style game with a fractured twist. Unlike traditional objective paths, your team must destroy the enemy’s three “Hearts” — core crystals that hold the map together. With each one broken, you gain power... but lose something vital. Risk and reward have never been more intertwined.

Features

💔 Crystalline Hearts

Destroy three to win, but each one comes with a cost. Strategy meets sacrifice.

🕳️ No Traditional Jungle

Focused, lane-based combat. Say goodbye to just farming all game.

🧪 Item-Driven Depth

Inspired by Dota 2, items shape your path alongside XP scaling.

🧬 Hero Exposure Kits

Heroes can have unique effects triggered by the map’s shifting exposure phases.
