Game Overview

Fracturepoint is a MOBA-style game with a fractured twist. Unlike traditional objective paths, your team must destroy the enemy’s three “Hearts” — core crystals that hold the map together. With each one broken, you gain power... but lose something vital. Risk and reward have never been more intertwined.

Features

  • Three-Heart destruction objective system
  • No traditional jungle — tighter team lanes
  • Item system inspired by Dota 2
  • Unique hero kits with exposure mechanics
